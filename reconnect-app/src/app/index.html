Project Inspirations


This project is inspired by how we experience sound and images in our lives.Under usual circumstances, our brains process these two aspects of information together.


What this project aims to do is to break the connection between sound and images and rebuild the link using our original ideas.


It is an attempt to disrupt memory, sensory experiences, and explore more possibilities of imagination.


Try to upload a video under "Home" page, and our website would help erase the background sound and generate new music for your video.


It could be a video taken in your daily life, or it could be a movie clip. Let's reinvent sound!
